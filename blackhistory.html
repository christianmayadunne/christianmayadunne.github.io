Black History Month: Bob Marley

Bob Marley was a Jamaican Reggae singer and songwriter; he sold over 20 million records throughout his life time and served as the ambassador for Reggae on a worldwide stage. Bob Marley is one of my heroes because of his philosophy and message.

Reasons Bob Marley is a significant Black History ambassador

1. He spread a message of love and peace through his music

2. He gave a voice to an opressed, marginalized population

3. He stood up in the face of challenges

 
  • Bob Marley's real name is Nesta Bob Marley
  • Bob Marley grew up in Nine Mile, a district in St Ann Parish, Jamaica.
  • Bob Marley has his own coffee brand
